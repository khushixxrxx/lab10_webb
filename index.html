Transformations

The Twist

This wedding was a whole structural curve steel hung up with flowers and screens, Baz team thought out of the box and challenged themselves to deliver a structural wedding that have never seen before. A wedding that everyone has to think about constructing it 100 times before even dare. Light and screens were defining the beauty of this architectural structure, reflecting the space and the scale.

Splendid Forest

We designed the event area to solely mirror the sensuality of being in a forest, fully surrounded by various different kinds of trees and bountiful flower plants. Our colors ranged from tones of cream shade selections to calm the mood of the chosen palette of greens and creams, and in between our built structures, we sprinkled iconic tropical touches of trees that embraced the guests as they mingled around the venue to chit and chat.

Frozen wedding

Our inspiration started from the white winter days, we brought to life the experience of exposing a casual snowy day, using visuals around a built-in white castle. The path sparkles and crunches like sugar underfoot, and the coolness brings the guests right into the now, into the moment of blasts and joy. Though the flowers sleep down the ceilings and the trees show their lofty arms, once more, a smile plays upon these cold harmonies. For as much as we love winter time, we love the snow more. We made sure the guests truly lived the experience of a cold frozen winter wedding.
